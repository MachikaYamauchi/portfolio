Our Story

A healthy community is built on healthy food. And healthy food depends on how it's grown, harvested and prepared. We think the question “where did your food come from?” is one each of us should be able to answer.

Bench Bakery share the tools, education and community people need to access, cook and eat nutritious, whole food. And the more people crave traceable grains, the more family farmers we can partner with.

Traceable food is trustworthy food. Clarity begets health. We focus on grains because that's where we can make the most impact. It's a cornerstone of our kitchens. Grains allow us to go big—to give a lot of people access to nourishing food.
